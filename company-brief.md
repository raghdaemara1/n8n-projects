# FPT Software — Interview Intelligence Brief

## Who They Are

FPT Software is the technology services arm of FPT Corporation, Vietnam's largest publicly listed technology conglomerate. With USD 1.34 billion in revenue (2025) and 33,000+ employees across 30+ countries, they are one of Asia's most significant IT outsourcing and digital transformation companies. Their client roster includes 130+ Fortune Global 500 companies across Aviation, Automotive, BFSI, Healthcare, Logistics, Manufacturing, Utilities, and more.

---

## What They Actually Do (Plain English)

FPT Software is hired by large enterprises to **build, automate, and modernise their technology systems**. Think of them as a high-scale digital transformation factory — clients bring complex, legacy-heavy operations and FPT provides the engineering talent, AI tooling, and delivery frameworks to modernise them.

Their core offerings:
- **IT Outsourcing** — Staffing and managing engineering teams for global enterprises
- **Digital Transformation** — Migrating legacy systems, building new digital products
- **AI & Automation** — Injecting AI into client workflows and building intelligent systems
- **Cloud & IoT** — Cloud-native migrations and connected device platforms
- **Analytics** — Data platforms, dashboards, and ML-powered decision support

---

## The AI-First Pivot (Why This Role Exists)

In 2024–2025, FPT made a bold strategic bet: **"All In on AI"**. Key moves include:

- **FleziPT Platform** — Their proprietary AI-first platform for enterprise transformation, featuring tools like AgentVista (AI agent management), CodeVista (AI-powered dev), and TestVista (AI testing)
- **FPT AI Factory** — A joint initiative with NVIDIA for enterprise AI infrastructure
- **50,000 AI Experts by 2030** — They are aggressively upskilling their workforce; a Senior AI Engineer with n8n expertise slots directly into this growth agenda
- **Strategic Partnerships** — Microsoft, SAP, NVIDIA, Landing AI, Mila (Quebec AI Institute)
- **CES 2026** — Showcased AI-first innovations, signalling serious global ambition

The hiring of a Senior AI Engineer with n8n experience tells you they are building **internal automation infrastructure** and **client-facing AI workflow solutions**. They need someone who can architect, build, and deliver production-grade n8n pipelines — not just prototype them.

---

## Why n8n Specifically?

n8n is a low-code/no-code workflow automation platform that FPT almost certainly uses to:
1. Rapidly prototype and deploy automation workflows for clients
2. Integrate disparate enterprise systems (CRMs, ERPs, ticketing, communication tools) without heavy custom code
3. Build AI-augmented automation (connecting OpenAI/Gemini/Claude to business workflows)
4. Enable non-engineers to maintain and modify automations post-delivery

Senior-level n8n at FPT likely means: architecting reusable workflow patterns, managing credentials and error handling at scale, integrating with enterprise APIs, building AI agent pipelines, and possibly leading a small team.

## What They Value in Candidates

Based on their AI-first posture and scale, they will look for:

- **Production mindset** — Not just "it works in dev" but error handling, retries, logging, monitoring
- **Systems thinking** — Understanding how n8n fits into a larger enterprise architecture
- **AI integration depth** — LLMs, embeddings, vector databases, agents — not just basic API calls
- **Client-facing communication** — FPT delivers for clients, so you need to articulate technical decisions
- **Speed and delivery** — FPT's competitive advantage is delivery pace; they want builders, not theorists
- **Agentic AI awareness** — Multi-agent systems, tool use, orchestration frameworks (n8n AI nodes, LangChain, ADK)

FPT competes with Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, and Capgemini for digital transformation contracts. Their differentiator is **AI-speed + Vietnam-based cost efficiency + global delivery**. If you join as a Senior AI Engineer, your work will likely be deployed directly into client projects at enterprise scale.
